# Understanding Your Menstrual Cycle

The menstrual cycle is a natural process that prepares the body for a possible pregnancy. Understanding your cycle can help you track your health and plan your activities.

## The Four Phases
1.  **Menstruation**: The shedding of the uterine lining (the period).
2.  **Follicular Phase**: The body prepares to release an egg.
3.  **Ovulation**: The egg is released from the ovary.
4.  **Luteal Phase**: The body waits to see if the egg is fertilized.

### 💡 Quick Tips:
*   Track your cycle using a calendar or an app.
*   Eat iron-rich foods like spinach and beans during your period.
*   Stay active—light exercise can help with cramps!
